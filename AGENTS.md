# CV

## Purpose

Produce an academic CV suitable for:

- faculty applications
- postdoctoral applications
- grant applications
- conference submissions

## Formatting

Maintain consistent formatting.

Never manually adjust spacing to fit pages.

## Writing

Use factual language only.

Never exaggerate achievements.

Prefer action verbs.

## Publications

Preserve publication order.

Do not invent DOIs.

Do not modify author ordering.

## Dates

Use YYYY or Month YYYY consistently.

## Before committing

Compile the PDF.

Ensure there are:

- no LaTeX warnings
- no overfull boxes
- no missing references
